## ⚠️ Common Rejection Reasons

### 1. Insufficient Content
**Problem**: Not enough articles or low-quality content
**Solution**: Write 20-30 quality articles before applying

### 2. Copied Content
**Problem**: Content copied from other websites
**Solution**: Write 100% original content

### 3. Policy Violations
**Problem**: Content violates AdSense policies
**Solution**: Review all content and remove anything prohibited

### 4. Navigation Issues
**Problem**: Difficult to navigate or broken links
**Solution**: Ensure all menu items work and site is easy to use

### 5. Under Construction
**Problem**: Site looks incomplete or has placeholder content
**Solution**: Complete all pages and remove "coming soon" messages

### 6. Low Traffic
**Problem**: Not enough visitors
**Solution**: Build traffic through SEO and social media (aim for 50+ daily visitors)
